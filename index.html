Mary White - LCSW

I am dedicated to supporting survivors of sexual abuse and helping them navigate the healing process. With a focus on trauma-informed care and client empowerment, I strive to provide a safe and compassionate environment for all my clients.

I have been working in this field for over 20 years, starting my journey in New York City. My experience spans various settings including crisis intervention, long-term recovery, and private practice. I have supervised and trained numerous professionals, ensuring high standards of care and support for survivors. My goal is to help clients regain control of their lives and find hope and strength in their journey towards healing.

My Approach

As a Licensed Clinical Social Worker based in Washington, I employ evidence-based strategies to support survivors in their healing journey. I work with individuals aged 18 and above who are dealing with the aftermath of sexual abuse. My approach combines research-based techniques with empathy and understanding, aiming to foster resilience and a sense of empowerment in my clients.
